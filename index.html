Know the CAT Exam

Everything you need to know about the Common Admission Test.

Eligibility Criteria
  • Degree: Bachelor's Degree in any discipline.
  • Marks: Min 50% (General/OBC) or 45% (SC/ST/PwD).
  • Final Year: Students in final year can also apply.
Marking Scheme
+3 Correct
-1 Wrong
0 Skip

* No negative marks for TITA questions.

Exam Pattern Overview
Section Duration No. of Questions (Approx)
VARC (Verbal Ability & Reading Comp) 40 mins 24
DILR (Data Int. & Logical Reasoning) 40 mins 22
QA (Quantitative Ability) 40 mins 22
Total Exam 120 mins ~68 Questions
Syllabus Overview
Section Name Detailed Topics High Weightage Areas
VARC
  • Reading Comprehension: Passages, Tone, Inference
  • Verbal Ability: Para Jumbles, Summary, Odd One Out
 Focus: RC is ~70% of section.
DILR
  • Data Interpretation: Tables, Charts, Caselets
  • Logical Reasoning: Arrangements, Games, Relations
 Focus: Selection of sets is key.
Quant (QA)
  • Arithmetic: Percentages, TSD, Time & Work
  • Algebra: Equations, Logarithms
  • Geometry & Modern Math: Triangles, Probability
 Focus: Arithmetic covers ~60%.
